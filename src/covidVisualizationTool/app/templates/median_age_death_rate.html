{% block title %} How Did the Median Age of Each Country Affect the Total Deaths? {% endblock %}

{{ title }}
The purpose of these charts is to answer the question of "Does a higher median age mean more COVID-19 deaths?" COVID-19 has been known to affect older populations significantly more. Thus, it is statistically evident that countries with a higher median age experienced more deaths. It's important to note that the death counts can include probable deaths (as in COVID-19 was the probable cause of death), where reported. In the chart below, the grey shaded bubbles represent different countries. When moused over, the median age and total deaths per million are shown for each country.
