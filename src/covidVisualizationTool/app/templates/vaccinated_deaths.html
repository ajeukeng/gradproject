{% block title %} How Did the Number of People Vaccinated and Boosted Affect the Number of Deaths?{% endblock %}

Vaccinated and Boosted Death Rate
The purpose of these charts is to answer the question of "Did the death rate from COVID-19 decrease when more people were partially vaccinated, fully vaccinated, or boosted?" The number of deaths are the total number of deaths per day attributed to COVID-19. Confirmed deaths are collected by Johns Hopkins University by date of report, rather than the date of death. Therefore, the number reported on a given day does not necessarily represent the actual number on that date, because of the long reporting chain that exists between a new death and its inclusion in statistics. This also means that time series can show sudden changes (negative or positive) when a country corrects historical data, because it had previously under- or overestimated the number of deaths. It's important to note that the death counts can include probable deaths, where reported. These charts only feature data from the United States.
