{% block title %} Covid Visualization Tool {% endblock %}

Many of the charts featured in the Covid Visualization Tool use different scales. This is because of the large range of data. For example, vaccinations were measured by millions while positivity rate was measured in percentages. For this reason, it was not possible to have multiple graphs in one chart.

Correlation between death rate, vaccinations, and boosters

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the number of partially vaccinated, fully vaccinated, and boosted on the death rate. It was assumed that if more people are vaccinated and boosted, there would be fewer deaths.

Correlation between median age and death rate

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the median age of a country on the death rate. It was assumed that countries with a higher median age experienced a higher death rate.

Correlation between Covid-19 test positivity rate and total tests

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the number of Covid-19 tests taken and the number of positive tests. It was assumed that when more Covid-19 tests are administered the positivity rate of these tests increases.

Correlation between ICU patients and vaccinations

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the number of people vaccinated and the number of ICU patients with a confirmed COVID-19 case. It was assumed that if more people are vaccinated there would be fewer patients in the ICU with COVID-19.

Correlation between the number of people boosted and the COVID-19 test positivity rate

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the number of people boosted and the COVID-19 test positivity rate. It was assumed that if more people were boosted, fewer people will be infected with COVID-19.

Correlation between total population and number of vaccinations

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of population size of the likeliness of vaccination. It was assumed that countries with a smaller population had a higher vaccination rate because of groupthink.

Correlation between lockdowns, deaths, and cases

The purpose of this visualization was to understand the affect of the lockdowns on the total number of deaths and cases a country experienced. It was assumed that countries with more strict lockdowns, experienced fewer deaths and cases.
